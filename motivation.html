Motivation Factor

Is to bring total awareness to the rapidly increasing Nationwide Homeless Epidemic In effort to suppress & eventually eradicate this problem by providing Food & Clothing ,in addition to help obtaining Shelter ,Housing ,Mental Health & Substance Abuse Treatment services to our communities Nation & eventually World Wide. We truly want our citizens to know that there's someone(s)out here in this world that may not know them personally; however they do love them due to the fact that they can sympathize & empathize with your plight because we too have experienced the same or similar hardships in our own lives. Therefore by working together hand in hand we will be able to put an end to this problem & create a better world for the future.
